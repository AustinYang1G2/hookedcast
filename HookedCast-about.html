About

Why CastRight exists

Most gear guides either dump 40 options on you or push whatever's on sale. CastRight asks three questions and gives you one specific, honest answer — the setup we'd actually recommend to a friend.

It's a small, independent project, built and maintained by one person who fishes and codes. No sponsorships baked into the recommendations, no accounts, no tracking beyond what's needed to remember your last quiz answers on your own device.

Have a setup you think should be added, or a correction? Get in touch.
